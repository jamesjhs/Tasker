💡 1. What does Tasker do?

Tasker is a simple timer and record-keeper for your working day. Each time you start a piece of work, you tap a button — Tasker starts the clock. When you finish, you tap stop and choose a couple of options to describe what the task was. Over time you build up a picture of where your time goes.

⏱️
Built-in stopwatch
Automatically times each task — no mental arithmetic needed.
📊
Instant charts
See exactly how you spent your day the moment you finish.
🔒
Fully anonymous
No names, no patient details — just task types and times.
📥
Download your data
Export a spreadsheet of your records any time you like.
⛔
Never type patient names, initials, NHS numbers, or anything that could identify a person. Record task type only — not who was involved.
🔑 2. Creating your account & logging in

First time? Choose one of these routes:

🙋
Self-register
Tap "Don't have an account? Register" on the sign-in screen, choose a password, and accept the data policy.
👩‍💼
Admin-created
Your administrator gives you a username and a one-time password. You set your own permanent password at first sign-in.
✍️
Write down your username immediately. It is shown only once. It is two random words joined together (for example SilverMango) — it is not your real name and cannot be recovered if lost.

Signing in — three quick steps

1
Type your username in the box (upper or lower case is fine).
2
Type your password. Tap the 👁️ icon to check what you typed.
3
Tap Log in. You will land on the home screen.

⏸️ 6. If you get interrupted

Interruptions happen! While a task is running, tap ⏸️ Interrupted. A menu appears with three choices:

▶
Resume — continue from here The interruption is already over. Keep the clock going. The time away is not counted.
📝
Enter interruption times manually Type in when it started and ended so that exact time is removed from the task total.
🗑
Discard this task Throw the whole task away and start fresh.
ℹ️
You can record more than one interruption on the same task. Each one will appear on the review screen, and you can delete any added by mistake before saving.

📊 Analytics screen

  • A breakdown of today's tasks by type and time spent.
  • A bar chart of My Group vs Personal time.
  • Tap 📅 Long-term History to see up to 30 days of records.
  • Filter by date, task type, or outcome using the filter bar.
  • Tap ✏️ Edit on any task to correct its details.

⏳
All task records are automatically deleted after 30 days. If you want to keep your data, download the spreadsheet before then.
🔒 9. Your privacy
  • Your username is random words — not your real name.
  • Your password is stored in a form no one can read back, including the administrator.
  • The administrator can see how many users exist and how many tasks have been recorded — but not any task details, timings, or flags.
  • No data is shared with any outside organisation.
  • Records are deleted automatically after 30 days.
⛔
Free-text notes have been removed. Task annotations use only structured flag options from a predefined list, so no personal data can be accidentally typed. If you have any concern about data, contact your administrator immediately.

For full details, see the Data & Use Policy.

👥 Teams in the system

When you first log in you will be asked to pick the team that best matches your role:

👨‍⚕️ Doctors 🩺 HCAs 👩‍⚕️ Nurses 👩‍💼 Patient-facing admin 💊 Pharmacy/Dispensary 📝 Secretarial
ℹ️
Your team determines which Task From, Task Type, and Outcome options are shown by default. You can then untick any options you never use from ⚙️ Settings → Customise My Options.
