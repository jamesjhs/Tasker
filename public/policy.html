📋 Tasker — Data and Use Policy

Version 1.12.2 — Updated April 2026

⚠️ CRITICAL: You must NEVER enter any patient names, initials, NHS numbers, dates of birth, staff names, or any other information that could identify a patient or staff member. This application is for recording task metadata only.

1. About This Application

Tasker is a personal task-logging tool for healthcare staff to track their workload activity anonymously. It is hosted on a private, personal server and is not connected to any NHS system.

2. What Data Is Collected

  • Anonymous username — a computer-generated, two-word identifier (e.g. CalmRiver). Not linked to your real name.
  • Password — stored as a one-way cryptographic hash. Never readable by anyone, including the administrator.
  • Task metadata — type (duty/personal), start and end times, category, subcategory, outcome, and interruption times.
  • Task flags — structured, administrator-defined annotations selected from a predefined list (e.g. "Sent to wrong user"). No free-text is stored.
  • Anonymous event counts — total login events, task counts etc. No user identity or data is attached to these counts.

3. What Is NEVER Collected

  • Real names, surnames, or any personal identifier — including staff names and initials
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • NHS numbers, hospital numbers, or any patient identifier
  • Staff identification numbers, initials, or role-specific identifiers
  • Patient names, initials, ward names, or any information that could identify a patient
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Free-text notes or free-text suggestions — these are transmitted by email to the administrator and are never stored on the server
ℹ️ Free-text notes have been removed from v1.8.1. Task annotations use structured flag options only. Dropdown and flag suggestions are emailed directly to the administrator and are never stored on this server.

4. Data Retention

Task records are automatically and permanently deleted after 30 days. This deletion is irreversible and runs automatically every day. Your account (username and password) is retained until you request deletion or an administrator removes your account.

5. Who Can See What

  • You — can see and export only your own task data. You cannot see any other user's data.
  • Administrator — can see only the total number of registered users and total event counts. The administrator cannot access your task data, timestamps, or analytics. The administrator can see your username for the purpose of account management.
  • No one else — data is stored only on the server hosting this application and is not shared with any third party, cloud service, or NHS system.

6. Access

This application is accessible from mobile devices and desktop browsers.

7. Your Responsibilities

  • You must NEVER enter patient names, initials, identifiers, or any information that could identify a patient.
  • You must NEVER enter staff names, initials, role identifiers, or any information that could identify a colleague.
  • You are responsible for keeping your username and password secure.
  • You should log out when you have finished your session.
  • You are responsible for keeping your device and network connection secure when using this application.
  • If you believe you have accidentally entered patient-identifiable or staff-identifiable information, contact the administrator immediately so your account data can be deleted.

8. Right to Deletion

You have the right to request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Contact the administrator to do so. The administrator can permanently delete your account and all your task data, which is irreversible.

9. Security

  • All sessions are protected with CSRF tokens and rate limiting.
  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12.
  • Session cookies are HTTP-only and SameSite=strict.
  • Sessions automatically expire after 30 minutes of inactivity and cannot span midnight.
  • The server uses HTTPS in production (the administrator is responsible for ensuring this).

10. No Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties, cloud services, analytics providers, or any external system. The only external resource loaded is Chart.js from a CDN (for charts); no user data is sent to the CDN.

11. ICO Registration

This application is operated by a data officer registered with the UK's Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). The registered data officer is jamesjhs and the registered organisation is jahosi.co.uk. Processing of personal data under this application is conducted in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to request deletion of your data, contact the administrator who provided you with your account credentials.

This policy was last updated at the time of deployment. The administrator is responsible for reviewing and updating it as needed.
